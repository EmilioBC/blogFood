Pork Stew

Pork stew is mainly a food haven for carnivorous like me. Naturally I really like pork and cooking it in the way of a stew makes it more special. This is one of my favorite dishes and this is best served with rice and Cocacola drink.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Kilo pork
  • 1 Chopped onions
  • 4 Cloves of chopped garlic
  • Salt
  • Soysauce
  • Vinegar
  • Black pepper
  • Cutting Board icon by Icons8

Steps:

  1. Heat the pan and add oil.
  2. Put the garlic into the pan and sautee it until it turn into golden brown.🧄
  3. Add the onions and spread a bit of salt on top and continue stirring the garlic and onions.🧅
  4. When the onions looks moist and hald cooked, you can then add the port and mix again.🐷
  5. When the meat looks white, you can then add the soysauce and vinegar depending on the level of the taste you prefer. Some people prefer it salty and some prefer it sour.
  6. Cover the pan on medium heat and let it boil until the water goes down halfway the pan.🥘
  7. Once you see that the soup looks sticky, then it is time to enjoy your pork stew!🍘

This pork stew should look a bit brownish due to the mixture of soysauce and vinegar. It will have a slight scent of onions depending on how much you put in it. Lastly, the taste of this pork stew varies from one person to another which is why whoever cook this will always be perfect for their taste buds.
